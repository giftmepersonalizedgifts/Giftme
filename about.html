Our Story of Emotional Gifting

GiftMe was born from a simple belief: every gift should tell a story, capture an emotion, and create a memory that lasts forever. We're not just selling products; we're facilitating meaningful connections through personalized expressions of love, gratitude, and celebration.

Our Mission

Transforming Gift-Giving Into Emotional Experiences

At GiftMe, we believe that the most precious gifts aren't the most expensive ones, but those that carry personal meaning and emotional value. Our mission is to empower everyone to create gifts that truly matter - gifts that speak from the heart and create lasting memories.

We're building more than an e-commerce platform; we're creating a space where creativity meets emotion, where every gift becomes a vessel for love, appreciation, and human connection.

"Every gift tells a story"

- GiftMe Philosophy

Our Vision

A World Where Every Gift is Meaningful

We envision a future where gift-giving transcends material value and becomes a genuine expression of human connection. Where people don't just give things, but share experiences, memories, and emotions through thoughtfully crafted presents.

Our vision extends beyond today's marketplace. We're working toward becoming the global platform for emotional gifting, where millions can create, share, and cherish personalized gifts that make life's special moments unforgettable.

Our Journey

From a simple idea to a platform transforming how people give and receive gifts

2018

The Inspiration

Founder Sarah Chen struggled to find meaningful gifts for her grandmother's 80th birthday. This sparked the idea for a platform where anyone could create personalized, emotional gifts.

2019

First Prototype

Launched a basic web app allowing users to customize mugs and t-shirts. Received overwhelming positive feedback from early users who loved the emotional connection.

2020

Platform Launch

Officially launched GiftMe with expanded product categories and the innovative "Emotional Gifts" section featuring timed messages and video greetings.

2023 - Present

Global Community

Served over 50,000 customers worldwide, expanded to 15+ countries, and introduced AI-assisted gift design tools. Continuing to innovate emotional gifting experiences.
